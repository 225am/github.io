rVenues was founded by devoted parents of a NCAA Division 1 student athlete and a die-hard college sports fan who have experienced the anxiety and frustration of losing their way to an away game on an unfamiliar campus. That’s because most sports fields do not have an address that visitors can use to find the field. Many hours and gallons of gas have been wasted because there is no consistent, reliable source for this information – until now. rVenues is the most complete and comprehensive directory of sports venues available in easy to use mobile apps. rVenues currently offers sports locations/directions for the NFL, MLB a well as NCAA D1, D2 and D3 venues by sport and college/school.

Athletic Departments, Coaches, Officials, Team Parents and Alumni

Our library of sports venues is indispensible. Athletic Directors (AD), Sports Information Directors (SID), baseball coaches, field hockey officials and football fans finally have access to accurate, standardized locations and directions nationwide, in the palm of their hands. Know where every home and away field, stadium, area, court and rink is located. Have a complete set of venues by sport at your fingertips. Save time. Save gas. Save your nerves.
